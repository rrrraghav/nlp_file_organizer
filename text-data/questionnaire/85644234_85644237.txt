Hello, I’m	of Tucson's Action Committee
and we are talking with residents of the City of Tucson
about the coming General Election to be held on November 5.

There are ten propositions on the ballot where Tucsonans
will be asked to decide YES or NO.

We are seeking opinions from the registered voters on these
issues and we need your assistance.

(1) Proposition 100 is an initiative
proposal called the Neighborhood
Protection Amendment. It requires
voter approval for any parkway,
freeway, or grade separated inter-
section before tax money can be
spent for construction.

Do you favor or oppose this measure?	Favor.......1	69%
					Oppose......2	18%
					No Opinion...3	13%



(2) Proposition 101 raises the property
tax limitation within the City of
Tucson from $1.25 per $100 assessed
valuation to $1.75 per $100.

Do you favor or oppose this measure?	Favor.......1	16%
					Oppose......2	62%
					No Opinion...3	22%



(3) Proposition 102 is a use tax of 2%
requiring city residents to pay the
2% sales tax on purchases made
outside the city limits.

Do you favor or oppose this measure?	Favor.......1	19%
					Oppose......2	69%
					No Opinion...3	13%
