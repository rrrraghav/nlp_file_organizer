PSA 12/96 Teen Qualitative Topic Guide (Final)

Warm-up 8 min (8)

Intro self.
We will be talking about advertising.
We are going to be looking at some commercials and I want to hear what you think about them.

Point out/explain apparatus; mikes, window
What are some of your favorite/least commercials? Why? (re-word - too evaluative)
Seen any commercials on health or safety issues? Tell me about them
Have any TV spots ever changed how you felt about something? How?

First Commercial 2 min (10)
Here is the first commercial we’ll be looking at - I'm going to play it twice.
Just watch it, and we will talk about it when you’re through.
Play the commercial twice

Fill out Questionnaire 10 min (20)

Hand out the questionnaire.
Fill these out first, and then we will discuss it.
If you have trouble answering any of the questions, put a check mark
next to it, and we will go back and talk about it later.
Have them fill out questionnaire.
Observe them carefully - for signs of confusion
frustration, boredom - and note where they occur.

Discuss Questionnaire 12 min (32)

Lets talk about the questionnaire first
What was it like? (Clear/confusing, easy/hard, interesting/boring, ...)
What parts need fixing? Tell me about them
How could it be made better? (clearer, easier, less boring, more relevant)
What questions seem repetitive... like they are asking you the same
thing in different words?
What other adjectives would you use?
What other questions would you ask?

Discuss Commercial 10 min (42)

Familiarity. Seen it before?
Recall/comprehension. Tell me about the commercial…
Probe recall - look for affect
Intent. What do you think it was trying to do?
Effect. Does it do it?
Appeal. Would you pay attention to it if it played in a show you were watching?
Target. Who is it aimed at? Will it reach them?
