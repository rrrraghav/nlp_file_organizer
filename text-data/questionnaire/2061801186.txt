POTENTIAL CONSUMER BOWLING QUESTIONS

1. How can I participate in Merit Bowling:
All league bowlers, 21 and older, at participating BPAA centers are automatically entered. Some participating BPAA centers will have a single night set aside for non-league bowlers to enter. To find out how to enter as a non-league bowler, please call the Bowling Proprietors Association of America at 1 (800) 343-1329.

2. Do I have to be a smoker to participate in Merit Bowling?
No. All league bowlers, 21 and older, at participating BPAA centers are automatically entered.

3. How do I know if my bowling center is participating in Merit Bowling?
Please tell me the name and town of your bowling center. Once respondent gives information, operator will look up the center among the list of participating centers.

4. Last year I recieved a buy 2 packs of Merit bowl a free game coupon. Are you running those coupons again this year? If so, where can I get them?
No, free game coupons are not available this year. You can, however, recieve money off coupons on Merit. If respondent is interested in the money off coupons, list the magazines and POS items containing coupons including the coupon values.
