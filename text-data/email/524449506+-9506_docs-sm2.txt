Date: January 1, 1996
TMA ID: T1960101.046
Source: Tobacco International—
Title: Star Tobacco’s 100% Natural Cigarette Buzz, Now Available
Location: Petersburg, Virginia

Star Tobacco has begun shipping Buzz, an exotic new 100% natural tobacco cigarette. Buzz has a “designer” filter and natural breath-freshening flavor produced by honey bees. Buzz is available in king size, 100s, full flavor, lights, and menthol. Buzz cigarettes represent the most significant development in cigarettes since the filter, said Samuel P. Sears, Jr., c.e.o.
