PUBLIC OPINION POLLS

The public clearly believes that smoking bans are not the right answer. Time and again, a clear majority prefer accommodation for smokers to smoking bars or no smoking restrictions in restaurants, workplaces, hotels and motels.

In restaurants

•	62 percent of respondents favored smoking areas set aside
(American Lung Association, 3/92)

•	66 percent preferred set aside smoking areas
(Gallup Poll Monthly, 12/91)

•	62 percent prefer separate smoking sections
(USA Today, 3/94)

In workplaces

•	64 percent of respondents favored smoking areas set aside
(American Lung Association, 3/92)

•	67 percent preferred set aside smoking areas
(Gallup Poll Monthly, 12/91)

•	68 percent prefer separate sections
(USA Today, 3/94)

•	64 percent prefer smoking and nonamoking sections
(Time Magazine, 4/94)

In hotels and motels

•	69 percent of respondents favored smoking areas set aside
(American Lung Association, 3/92)

•	70 percent preferred set aside smoking areas
(Gallup Poll Monthly, 12/91)
