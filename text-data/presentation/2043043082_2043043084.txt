BOYS AND GIRLS SHARE HOUSEHOLD TASKS
FINDS VIRGINIA SLIMS POLL

NEW YORK, N.Y. -- It has been widely documented that more women than ever before are establishing themselves in what were long held to be male bastions, including the professions and the sports world. And women today believe the same opportunities should be available for young girls.

According to The 1980 Virginia Slims American Women's Opinion Poll, the majority of women today believe that masculine and feminine ways of behavior are a result of the way people are raised and taught to act, rather than because of physical differences between the sexes. Consequently, it is not surprising to see that in larger numbers than ever before, the assignment of household chores is based on criteria other than sex.
