Key FDA Tobacco Rules
Affecting Retailers

Rules Effective February 28, 1997

•	Retailers may not sell cigarettes or smokeless tobacco to
anyone under 18 years of age.

•	Retailers must verify by photo ID that any person
purchasing cigarettes or smokeless tobacco is at least 18
years of age, unless premises are off-limits to persons
under 18 years of age.
