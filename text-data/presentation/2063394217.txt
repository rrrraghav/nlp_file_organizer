STATEMENT BY PHILIP MORRIS U.S.A.
IN RESPONSE TO THE NEW YORK TIMES STORY

NEW YORK, March 18, 1996 -- The fact that there are grand jury
investigations of the tobacco industry has been reported widely in the media.
While we strongly believe the company has acted lawfully, as a matter of policy
we do not comment on grand jury investigations.
