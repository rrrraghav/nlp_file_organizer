FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
November 5, 1992

New York, NY - Legal counsel for Philip Morris were informed today
that Marc Edell has voluntarily dismissed the Cipollone case. We have
also been informed that Mr. Edell and his co-counsel intend to dismiss
several other smoking and health cases in New Jersey in which they
have served as counsel. In addition, we understand that they will seek
permission from the courts to withdraw as counsel from the two
remaining cases in which the plaintiffs wish to proceed.
