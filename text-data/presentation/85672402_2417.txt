Presentation of Philip Witorsch to Committee
on Airliner Cabin Air Quality, National
Academy of Sciences, June 14, 1985

Dr. Chalmers, Members of the Committee:

My name is Philip Witorsch and I am a physician specializing
in Pulmonary Medicine. A copy of my curriculum vitae has been
made available to the committee.

I am presently Clinical Professor of Medicine in the
Division of Pulmonary Diseases and Allergy of the George Washing-
ton University Medical Center in Washington, D.C.

I am also currently Medical Director of the Center for
Environmental Health and Human Toxicology in Washington. The
Center works with both the public and private sector on problems
of environmental and occupational health and toxic substances.
