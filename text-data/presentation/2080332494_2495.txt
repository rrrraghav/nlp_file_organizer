Marlboro Chili Promotion: 2001

Objectives:
• Reinforce and further dimensionalize Marlboro’s flavor positioning and heritage
• Generate news and excitement among YAS

Strategy:
• Introduce multi-faceted program that:
    – provides immediate gratification and accessibility
    – reinforces the brand's core values
    – connects with YAS lifestyles through sociability

Rationale:
• Flavor cues associated with Chili closely represent Flavor of Marlboro
• Marlboro established “Food/Flavor” credentials with cookbooks and other food-focused programs
• Chili linked to sociability
