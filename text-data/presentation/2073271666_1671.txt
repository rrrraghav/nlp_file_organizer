RJR NABISCO REPORTS THIRD QUARTER RESULTS
(Highlights before extraordinary items in the 1993 and 1994 year-to-date periods)

Third Quarter:
• Fully Diluted Earnings Per Share up 175%, from $.01 to $.11
• Net Income up 192% to $216 million
• Operating Income up 57% to $678 million
• Cash Net Income up 67% to $347 million

Nine Months:
• Fully Diluted Earnings Per Share up 10% from $.31 to $.34
• Net Income up 41% to $602 million
• Operating Income up 17% to $1.99 billion
• Cash Net Income up 21% to $1.0 billion

NEW YORK, NEW YORK -- October 25, 1994 – RJR Nabisco Holdings Corp. (NYSE: RN) today announced results for the three- and nine-month periods ending September 30, 1994. The company said 1994 third quarter net income of $216 million was 192 percent above net income of $74 million in the comparable 1993 quarter. Net income per common share, on a fully diluted basis, was $.11 in the 1994 third quarter, up 175 percent from $.04 per common share recorded in last year’s third quarter.

Operating income in the 1994 third quarter was $678 million, up 57 percent from 1993 third quarter operating income of $431 million. Net sales were $3.97 billion, a 10 percent increase from net sales of $3.60 billion in the third quarter last year.

Cash net income (net income before after-tax amortization of trademarks and goodwill) for the third quarter was $347 million, up 67 percent from cash net income of $208 million in the prior year’s quarter.
