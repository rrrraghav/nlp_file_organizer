PARAMETER

A PROCESS OR PRODUCT CHARACTERISTIC THAT VARIES AND WHOSE PERFORMANCE
IS BELIEVED TO DIRECTLY OR INDIRECTLY INFLUENCE PROCESS OR PRODUCT QUALITY..

CONTROLLABLE

BASED ON THE CHARTED RESULTS THERE ARE DEFINITE ACTIONS ON THE "PROCESS"
THAT CAN BE TAKEN BY (PREFERABLY) THE PERSON DOING THE CHARTING OR OTHERS.

STANDARDIZED CONTROL MOVES

FOR EACH CONTROLLABLE PARAMETER, AN AGREED TO, PRIORITIZED SEQUENCE OF
ACTIONS (i.e. PROCESS CHECKS, SETTING CHANGES, COMMUNICATIONS, etc.)
THAT WILL BE FOLLOWED BY AN OPERATOR FOR THE PURPOSE OF BRINGING THAT
PARAMETER BACK IN CONTROL MOST EFFICIENTLY WITHOUT ADVERSELY EFFECTING
OTHER IMPORTANT PARAMETERS...
