THE BEACON Journal May 27, 1983

SPORTS

TIMBERDOODLES

GOOD EATING

BAKED SMALLMOUTHS/BASS

1 pound bass or smallmouth
½ green peppers, chopped
1 onion, sliced
1 can mushrooms (drained)
½ cup mayonnaise
Salt and pepper to taste

Mix all ingredients except bass. Stuff
bass with mixture. Bake in pan of water
at 350 degrees for 45 minutes or until
fish flakes easily. Serve with a pat of
butter.

FISHING REPORT

Fishing has been good in most areas of
the state. Walleye are hitting well in Lake
Erie. Bass fishing is fair to good in inland
lakes and rivers. Panfish are plentiful.
Crappie action is improving. Catfish are
biting on live bait.

HUNTING REPORT

Turkey hunting season opened May 10th.
Reports have been mixed, with some hunters
having success and others coming up empty.
Squirrel hunting is good in most areas.

FISHING LICENSE INFORMATION

Ohio residents: $25.00
Nonresidents: $39.00
Senior citizens (66 or older): $12.50

Licenses are available at most bait and
tackle shops, sporting goods stores and
county clerks’ offices.

HUNTING LICENSES

Hunting licenses are required for all
hunters 12 years of age or older. The fee
for resident hunting licenses is $18.00.
Nonresident hunting licenses are $45.00.

TRAININC OPPORTUNITIES

The Ohio Division of Wildlife offers a
variety of training programs throughout the
year. These programs include hunter safety,
waterfowl identification and trapping. For
more information, contact your local
Division of Wildlife office.
