Co-Owner Says
Mario Staying
With Parnell

WATKINS GLEN - Vel Mielich, co-owner of the Vel’s Parnell Jones race car which Mario Andretti drives in Formula One racing, has denied rumors that Andretti would leave the team to drive for a U.S.-based company.

“I don’t see how he could,” said Mielich. “We have a three-year contract with him and we plan to run the full Formula One circuit next year with Mario as our driver. We will run in the Indy 500, too. And unless will drive the full USAC circuit, I don’t see how he could be far from us. Everyone seems to think he knows more than I do.”

Mielich said that he and Jones will continue to look for sponsors. “We are looking for new sponsors,” said Mielich. “Viceroys a sponsor of the team now, thanks to Andy Hilldoerfer who represents the Andy Hildoerfer Racing Group. We’re running a 500 car a week ago at Long Beach.”

Andretti has been driving for the Parnell Jones team for three years.
