Daily Press

Tuesday, Aug. 8, 1995

REGION

MINOR DEVELOPMENT. Philip Mor-
ris U.S.A. has started manufacturing
panels and cartons of cigarettes marked
“Underage Sale Prohibited” at three of
its American plants. The notice is part
of the company’s “action against access”
initiative, announced in June. No word
Monday on when the new labels will
make their way into retail outlets.
