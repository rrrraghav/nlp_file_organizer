GENERAL OBJECTIVES AND SPECIFICATIONS

1.1 Objective

The objective of the intended study is to characterize (apparent
molecular weight, isoelectric point, antigenic sites)
the CYTOKERTINS of freshly isolated RNE and RTE cells, of RNE and RTE
cells in primary culture, and of a chemically transformed, focus-
derived RTE cell line by gel electrophoresis and immunoblotting.

1.2 Specifications

The intended investigation will provide basic information with
regard to the degree of homology between human and rat
cyto keratins - a class of intermediate filament proteins which
represent important markers of epithelial cell differentiation and
possible transformation-related changes.

The result may help to select specific antibodies as a tool for
further immunohistological examinations, such as concerning the
alterations that accompany metaplastic changes in the larynx, the
labeling of RTE cells in the chick embryo skin invasion assay, or
the differentiation of RTE and RNE cells in vitro.
