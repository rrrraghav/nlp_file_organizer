TEST FORM

A. INITIATOR OF TEST: J. SHARKEY

B. DATE SUBMITTED: 7/12/91

C. PURPOSE OF TEST: TO COLLECT DATA ON PRESS SYSTEM EFFICIENCY AFTER MAXIMIZING THE PRESS SYSTEM THROPUT RATE.
(PRESS EVALUATION TEAM)

D. TEST PROCEDURE AND SAMPLE COLLECTION/ANALYSIS:

1. ON 7/15/91, A PRELIMINARY TEST WILL BE CONDUCTED TO DETERMINE THE APPROPRIATE SET POINTS FOR KEY PRESS SYSTEM SEIS WHEN OPERATING THE PRESSES AT A MAXIMUM SUSTAINABLE THROPUT RATE.
THIS TEST WILL BE CONDUCTED ON THE LINE 1 ACB PRESS. THE TEAM ANTICIPATES THIS TEST TO LAST APPROXIMATELY 2-4 HOURS. NO ADDITIONAL SAMPLING WILL BE REQUIRED DURING THIS SET-UP PERIOD.
NOTE: DURING THIS AND FUTURE PRESS SYSTEM TESTING, ALL PROCESS STAGE STANDARDS WILL BE TEMPORARILY SUSPENDED.

2. ON 7/25 AT APPROXIMATELY 0700 HOURS, THE LINE 1 ACB PRESS LINE THROPUT RATES WILL BE SET AT THE RATE IDENTIFIED ON 7/15/91.
THE INCREASED THROPUT RATE SHOULD BE MAINTAINED FOR A MINIMUM OF 48 HOURS (TEST SHOULD END AT 0700 HOURS ON 7/17/91).

3. AFTER THE SYSTEM HAS STABILIZED, SAMPLING SHOULD BEGIN. SAMPLES SHOULD BE COLLECTED ONCE EVERY FOUR HOURS UNTIL A TOTAL OF 10 SAMPLE SETS HAVE BEEN COLLECTED (DEVIATION FROM THE FOUR HOUR FREQUENCY SHOULD BE AVOIDED). SAMPLES WILL BE COLLECTED FROM THE LINE 1 RUN PULPER AND FROM LINE I-3B PRESS.

4. SAMPLES SHOULD BE SEALED IN A 1 LITER CONTAINER AND REFRIG- ERATED UNTIL SAMPLE ANALYSIS CAN BE PERFORMED.

5. EACH SAMPLE SHOULD BE LABELED AS FOLLOWS:

DATE (TEST)
TIME:
SAMPLE LOC.:

6. AS TIME PERMITS, REMOVE A SINGLE SET FROM THE REFRIGERATOR AND PERFORM THE FOLLOWING ANALYSES:

SAMPLE LOCATION   ANALYSIS    REPLICATES
PULPER             TOTAL SOLIDS     2
3B PRESS CAKE      HWS              2
3B PRESS CAKE      TOTAL SOLIDS     2
