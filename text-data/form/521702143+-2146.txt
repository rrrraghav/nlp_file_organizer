SECTION IV FIRE AND EXPLOSION DATA
Flash Point: 150 Deg. F  (Seta Closed Cup)   Auto Ignition Temperature: 460 Deg. F
Extinguishing Media: Carbon Dioxide, Dry Chemical, Alcohol Foam. Use water spray to cool fire exposed containers.
Special Fire Fighting Procedures: Wear self-contained breathing apparatus and protective clothing to prevent contact with skin and eyes.
Unusual Fire & Explosion Hazards: Vapors may travel to a source of ignition and flash back.

SECTION V REACTIVITY DATA
Stability: Stable under normal conditions.
Conditions to Avoid: Excessive heat, sparks, open flame.
Incompatibility: Strong oxidizing agents.
Hazardous Decomposition Products: Carbon Monoxide, Carbon Dioxide.

SECTION VI HEALTH HAZARD DATA
Routes of Entry: Inhalation, Skin Contact, Eye Contact.
Acute Effects: May cause irritation to eyes, nose and throat. Prolonged or repeated skin contact may cause dermatitis.
Chronic Effects: No known chronic effects.

SECTION VII PRECAUTIONS FOR SAFE HANDLING AND USE
Eye Protection: Safety glasses with side shields are recommended.
Skin Protection: Wear impervious gloves and protective clothing to prevent skin contact.
Respiratory Protection: Use with adequate ventilation. If exposure limits are exceeded, use a NIOSH approved respirator appropriate for the concentration of vapors present.
Ventilation: Local exhaust ventilation is recommended to maintain vapor concentrations below exposure limits.
Storage & Handling: Keep containers tightly closed when not in use. Store in a cool, dry place away from heat and ignition sources.
Waste Disposal: Dispose of in accordance with federal, state and local regulations.
