11b. STATE FORMULA (Describe process when required by regulations)

A.P.F.X. (Processed) (0-4-03-A)

Alcohol (190 proof S.D.A. #4) used to extract the flavors
from angelica root by mechanical percolation. Each gallon
of finished flavor contains the extract of 4 pounds of
it. It contains approximately 90% alcohol which are
the mature content of the root and other substances with
the root.

The unfiltered Tobacco Mint flavoring is then mixed with
sufficient U.S. water to bring the proof down to 190
proofs as they are all mechanically combined. The flavor
is then used as an ingredient in tobacco products.

14. PROCESS (Describe in detail all steps in manufacturing)
See item 11b. Angelica root is mechanically percolated with
specially denatured alcohol (S.D.A. #4). The extract is then
mixed with U.S. water to bring the proof down to 190.
