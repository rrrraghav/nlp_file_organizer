GENERAL OBJECTIVE AND SPECIFICATIONS
P 0700/13/98

In the mainstream smoke (MS) of the cigarettes of the project 'Flavor' the following parameters will be determined:

TPM, nicotine, water in TPM, carbon monoxide, nitrogen oxides, formaldehyde, acetaldehyde, acrolein, propionaldehyde, phenolic compounds, hydrogen cyanide, 2-nitropyrane, 1,3butadione, isoprene, benzene, toluene, acrylonitrile, vinyl chloride, volatile and specific nitrosamines, N-nitroso derivatives, polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbons, benzo[a]pyrene, c-toluidine, 2-naphthyl amine, and 4-aminobiphenyl.

The yields of the X test cigarettes with benzyi-alcohol and vanillin will be compared to those of the control cigarette without flavor.

In addition, all parameters will be determined in MS of the reference cigarette 1RAF 4 used as an internal control.
