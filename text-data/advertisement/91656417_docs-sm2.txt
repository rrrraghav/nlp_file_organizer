Thieves Prefer Pontiac Firebird

If you buy a Pontiac Firebird or Chevy Camaro, you’re inviting thieves to pry out your car’s valuables. But if you prefer a Jaguar XJS or a Maserati Khamsin—well, you can afford it and neither of these are likely to be stolen.

Here are 30 models that have a lower-than-average risk (less than 1 in 100) of theft, according to the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration. Data covers cars from 1986 through 1992.
