Smoking Should Not Be A Part Of Growing Up

Part I: The Truth About Tobacco
Tobacco use is a serious health problem. It’s tough to quit once you start. This booklet explains the dangers of tobacco and gives you information to help you make healthy choices.

“Helping Youth Say No” – This part of a wide-ranging effort by many groups to discourage youth smoking. The industry has agreed to marketing codes and supports efforts to reduce teen smoking. State laws prohibit sales to minors, and the federal government has passed legislation to keep tobacco out of the hands of young people.
