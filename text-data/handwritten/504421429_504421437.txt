Flavors are used on Tobacco products to simplify, mellow and fortify the natural flavor of the processed tobacco leaf. Unlike most products to which flavors are added, tobacco products contain an abundance of natural flavor. However, in some instances this natural flavor may be of an undesirable nature or one which needs to be modified to meet the desire of the consumer; filer, since tobacco varies from crop to crop, some crops of Tobacco, depending on the growing season and varieties, are low in flavor and need fortifying.

Here, we are concerned with the flavoring of plug chewing tobaccos, sampling tobaccos and leafette.

Plug chewing tobaccos consist of a group of tobacco products which closely resemble food products from the standpoint of flavor in that the primary concern is with taste!
