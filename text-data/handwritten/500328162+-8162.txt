Dec 28.1953.
Erie Pa.

Dear Sir:

I am Submitting An Idea
To Stimulate The Sales of Cartels.
And also U.S. War Bonds, My Idea
is. That in so, seventy Thousands, of
Cartones, They Will Be one Dummy
Pack Holding The number for Bond.
When The smoker Knows he
might Receive Some Thing Free, That
Will switch To Cartels.

Yours Truly
Edward d. Metz
Y.M.C.A. 10th Peach St
Erie Pa.
