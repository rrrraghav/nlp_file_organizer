Don R.f. Rempuela

Well this is what writing
will be in the future &
hard to read.

I do not remember – have
forgotten most of the
September terms of sport
& continue sick at that.

Remember there Estate & like
Relative.

All names of those other
persons called “Uncle” being for-
gotten also.

Came forward person had
entire trial agreed in writing
“C’s” “C’s” “C’s” Remained price
& I suppose.

W. Supp bee be applied once
more for me to get her
in line of reproach to my
advantage there Remp & 1/10th.

Sader Samuel
Oct 2, 1879
Column of Class
