12/6/1890

R.J. Argyle Co
Gentlemen:

Thanks for the coupon for a painter.

I Camier. I feel y Canle gettin’ good time.
It tried my hand at to smoth things, but
it is too hard from this goods of lately while any day
after is gone until all comes along.

For many years until this month would
any Doctor told me that would would
hurty hill and as no one ages, I still
would be good, I don’t know any days or ways
to joy life. I spend money for cigarette then I
do for food.

It is an old letter more said in little manners,
I think yous statement every smallin’ farm stay.
the goods do with lasting wonders over.
I would admit strouse my childs dyank digging
hadeser-dales.

Believe me and thanks.
J. W. Brady
3818 Edmound
December 6th 1890
