CURRICULUM VITAE

Born July 19, 1896.

1917-1919 Medical studies at the University of Budapest.

1920-1923 Assistant at the Pathological Institute of the
University of Frankfurt-Main.

1923-1927 Assistant Professor (Privatdozent) of General Patho-
logy and Pathological Anatomy at the University of
Frankfurt-Main.

1927-1933 Associate Professor of General Pathology and Patho-
logical Anatomy at the University of Frankfurt-Main.

1933-1953 Professor of General Pathology and Pathological
Anatomy at the University of Istanbul.
Head of the Department of Pathology.
Director of the Pathological Institute.

In 1933 I was invited by the Turkish government to reorganize
the University of Istanbul and to establish a new Medical School
at the University of Istanbul.
