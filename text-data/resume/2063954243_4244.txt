A. James Alovis, Judge. Born in Los Angeles, California, June 19, 1936; admitted to the bar of California, 1962, and has practiced law in Santa Barbara County since that time.
Education: Loyola Univ., B.A., 1958; Loyola Law School, J.D., 1961.

Judge Alovis served as a Deputy District Attorney for Los Angeles County from 1961 to 1964, resulting in arrest and prosecution of 778 defendants, principally trial was 4-month notoriety trial of the “Hollywood Ten.” Specializing in Criminal Law. Admitted to Calif Bar Feb 21, 1962; U.S. District Court, Central Dist. of California, 1965; and to U.S. Supreme Court, November 1982. J.D., [CSU] [CJS], 1991 ("Continuing Viglance in Civil Procedure"), 1984 (“Criminal Law Update”).
