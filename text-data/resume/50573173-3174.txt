PERSONAL STATEMENT

I am an experienced physician with a strong background in nutrition sciences and medicine. I have dedicated my career to improving the nutritional status of patients with chronic diseases, particularly those requiring parenteral or enteral nutrition support. My research has focused on understanding the metabolic consequences of malnutrition and developing strategies to prevent and treat these complications. I am committed to providing high-quality patient care and training the next generation of nutrition professionals.
