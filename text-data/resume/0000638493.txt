EMILIO C. VENEZIAN

Dr. Venezian joined the staff of Arthur D. Little, Inc. in 1961. Since that time he has been engaged in a diversi.ty of projects dealing with epidemiology, the design and analysis of control systems, storage and retrieval of information, and economic problems in the fuel industry. His work has included the development of mathematical models of information retrieval systems and of large-scale computer systems. His epidemiological work has dealt with mathematical models of patterns of infection and with development of disease subsequent to infection.

Dr. Venezian received his B.Eng. in Chemical Engineering from McGill University in 1958. He continued his studies and received his doctorate at California Institute of Technology, specializing in the field of turbulent heat transfer. He is an associate member of the Operations Research Society of America, the Engineering Institute of Canada, and of Sigma Xi.
