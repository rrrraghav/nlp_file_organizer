CURRICULUM VITAE

Milton B. Rosenblatt, M.D.

1. 1931-1933 Internship and Residency in Pulmonary Diseases, Montefiore Hospital.

2. 1934 Fellowship, Pulmonary Division, Montefiore Hospital.

3. 1934-1951 Assistant, Associate, and Attending Physician, respectively, Pulmonary Division, Montefiore Hospital.

4. 1936-1956 Chief, Chest Clinic, New York City Hospital; Associate Visiting and Visiting Physician, respectively, New York City Hospital.

5. 1938-1940 Associate Visiting Physician, Municipal Sanatorium.

6. 1955 to Date-Chief, Medical Clinics, Metropolitan Hospital; Visiting Physician, Metropolitan Hospital and Bird S. Color Hospital.

7. Associate Attending Physician, Flower-Fifth Avenue Hospital.
8. Attending Physician, Doctors Hospital

9. 1935-1937 Assistant in Medicine and Instructor, Columbia University.

10. 1944 to Date-Associate Clinical Professor of Medicine, New York Medical College
