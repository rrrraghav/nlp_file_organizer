MERCOLA, Daniel, M.D., Ph.D. Associate As. Professor

University of California, Los Angeles BA 1963 Psychology
University of California, Los Angeles MS 1967 Biophysics
University of California, Los Angeles PhD 1969 Biophysics
University of Southampton, England BM 1981 Medicine

1969 - 1973: University of Oxford, postdoctoral fellow with Prof. Dorothy Hodgkin (Nobel Laureate)
1974 - 1979: University of Oxford, Member of Faculty of Agricultural and Biological Sciences,
(M.A.(Oxon), 1974).

1978 - 1981: University of Southampton, Medical studies, accelerated student
1982: California State License, M.D., No. A 4062
1981 - 1985: University of California at San Diego, Resident in Pathology
1985 Board Certification, American Board of Pathology, Anatomic Pathology, F.A.C.P.
1985 - present: University of California at San Diego, Department of Pathology, School of Medicine
