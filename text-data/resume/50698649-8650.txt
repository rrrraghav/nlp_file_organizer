Principal Investigator

RAZIN, Aharon Professor Tel Aviv, 1935

Education

Hebrew University of Jerusalem M.Sc. 1962 Biochemistry
Hebrew-Hadassah Medical School Ph.D. 1967
Thesis entitled "Interrelations in the metabolism of purine nucleotides in red blood cells”, written under the supervision of Prof. J. Magyar.

Professional experience

1969 - 1970 Fogarty Research fellow, Division of Biology, California Institute of Technology, with R.L. Sinsheimer.
1971 Senior lecturer in Biochemistry, The Hebrew University.
1971 Visiting Scientist, Medical Research Council, University of Cambridge, England with F. Sanger and J. Seearl.
1977 - 1978 Visiting Scientist in the City of Hope Medical Center, California, U.S.A., with A.D. Riggs.
1976 Associate Professor in Biochemistry, The Hebrew University.
1980 - 1984 Head, Department of Cellular Biochemistry, The Hebrew University School of Medicine.
1982 Professor of Biochemistry, The Hebrew University.
1981 - 1982 Head, Genetic Engineering Center, The Hebrew University.
1984 - 1986 Fogarty Scholar, Fogarty International Center, N.I.H.
1982 - present Editor, Biochimica Biophysica Acta.
1985 - 1989 Chairman, Institute of Biochemistry, The Hebrew University School of Medicine.
1988 Chair Jacob Grunbaum Professor of Medical Sciences, The Hebrew University.
