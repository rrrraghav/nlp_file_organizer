Investigator’s Curriculum Vitae and List of Publications

RINGO, John M.

Born 25 November 1943, Columbia, MO, USA

Degrees Earned:
A.B. Genetics 1969 U. California, Berkeley, CA
Ph.D. Genetics 1973 U. California, Davis, CA

Positions Held:
Asst. Professor of Zoology   U. of Maine, Orono, ME    1974-1980
Assoc. Professor of Zoology  U. of Maine, Orono, ME    1980-1988
Visiting Scientist            Brandeis U., Waltham, MA     1982-1985
Professor of Zoology         U. of Maine, Orono, ME    1988-
Scheinfeld Visiting Professor  Hebrew U., Jerusalem   1989

Fellowships and Externally Funded Grants:
NIH Predoctoral Traineeship (4 years) 1969-1973. I was sole PI for this grant, to investigate mechanisms of speciation.
NSF Research Project Grant (3 years) 1979-1981.
National Research Service Award for Senior Fellows from NIH (1 year) 1982-1983. This funded a year’s leave of absence, with Dr. Jeff Ball at Brandeis U.
Scheinfeld Visiting Professorship from The Scheinfeld Center for the Study of Human Genetics in the Social Sciences, Hebrew U. (1 year) 1989.  This funds a year's sabbatical leave, to work with Drs. Ruth Guttman (Hebrew U., Jerusalem) and Dan Segall (Tel Aviv U., Tel Aviv).
