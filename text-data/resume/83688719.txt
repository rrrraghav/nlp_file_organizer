Dr. Michael H. Popkin, Ph.D.

Dr. Michael H. Popkin, who received his Ph.D. in Counseling Psychology from
Georgia State University, is the author of more than 20 parenting books, including Active
Parenting of Teens and Active Parenting Today. Dr. Popkin is also the Founder and
President of Active Parenting Publishers, which has published ten best-selling parenting
video-based discussion programs.

Dr. Popkin has extensive media experience as a parenting expert, appearing on the
Oprah Winfrey Show as well as more than 100 national and local television programs. In
addition, he continues to be a featured speaker on effective parenting at conferences
throughout the world.
