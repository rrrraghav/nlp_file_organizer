Dear Doctor Hassaro: !

Upon the recommendation of the Scientific Advisory Board to the
Council For Tobacco research, a financial grant has been approved in the
amount of $39,870.00 for study of comparative racial prevalence of chronic
bronchitis*, in accordance with the application filed by you under date of
October 27, 1965.

It is understood that this grant is made subject to acceptance
by the appropriate institutional authorities.

The Board has taken into account your estimate that the project
will extend through two years, and it is anticipated that, following a re-
view of progress in the first year, and the receipt of an application for
renewal, prior consideration will be given to your needs in the allocation
of funds in the succeeding year.

Please refer to the Statement of Policy containing the condi-
tions and terms under which this above-mentioned grant is to be administered,
which is enclosed.

In preparing semiannual progress reports we will appreciate it
if you will use the format shown on the enclosed sample and furnish us with
15 copies. We would also like to be advised of any published papers result-
ing from this grant. Reprints are available we would appreciate 20
copies.

The Council For Tobacco Research will make an announcement of
this award in the Annual Report of the Scientific Director and in press re-
leases according to established practice.

Any questions of a scientific nature that may arise concerning
the project should be referred to Dr. Robert C. Hockett, Associate Scientific
Director, and we will appreciate your keeping him informed of progress at
intervals.

After discussing the approval of the project with the proper of-
ficials of your institution, will you please notify us of the date upon which it will
become active,



JFH/
