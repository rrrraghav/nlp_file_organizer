Gentlemen:

Reference is made to the agreement between you and us, on behalf of our client, The P. S. K. Schiefer Brewing Co., dated the 26th day of October, 1954, covering the furnishing by you to us of television time for the telecast of Brooklyn Dodgers baseball games.

With specific reference to Paragraph 11 of said agreement, this is to advise you that our client, The P. S. K. Schiefer Brewing Co., has submitted fifty percent (50%) of the rights under said agreement with respect to the 1955 season to The American Tobacco Company. We suggest your approval of said submission.

If you do approve, kindly sign the enclosed carbon copies of this letter and return same to us.

Very truly yours,

BATTTEN, BARTON, DURSTINE & OSBORN, INC.
