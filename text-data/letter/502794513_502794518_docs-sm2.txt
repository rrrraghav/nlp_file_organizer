B. DEVELOPMENT OF ANALYTICAL METHODS

1. Determination of Acids in Nonaqueous Solvents

a. Determination of Perchlorates

Additional perchlorates were assayed by the following procedure: Approximately 0.5 g. of sample was weighed into a 100-ml. volumetric flask and dissolved in 10 ml. of water. The solution was diluted to the mark with pyridine and 10 ml. aliquots were titrated potentiometrically with 0.1 N tetrabutylammonium hydroxide (Bu4NOH).
