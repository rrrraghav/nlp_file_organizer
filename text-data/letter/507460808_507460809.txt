SUBJECT: January, 1986 Magazine Premium Offer - Revision I

This will revise the previous brief for the subject promotion dated
10/4/85. The reason for the revision is the elimination of the pop-up
BIGIF coupon. All other elements remain as before. Abbe has been
alerted, by phone, of the change required.

BACKGROUND

CAMEL plans to execute a premium offer via selected young adult male
magazines. We will offer consumers a free CAMEL T-shirt via a toll
free phone number.

Respondents identified as non-CAMEL smokers, age 21-34, will receive
an unannounced store coupon good for two free cartons of any style
CAMEL. Those non-CAMEL smoker respondents, age 35+, will receive
six unannounced store coupons good for $2.00 off any style CAMEL
carton.

ASSIGNMENT

Develop creative and provide art/mechanicals for subject promotion.

CREATIVE CONSIDERATIONS

This confirms creative objectives, developed by Brand, and items
provided by Promotion.

CREATIVE OBJECTIVES:

   Maximize communication of the offer.
