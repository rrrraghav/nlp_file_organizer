This letter is to confirm the upcoming focus groups to be held at QCS in Overland Park, Kansas which you will be moderating.

Primary Research has been selected to provide recruiting, moderating, facilities, and refreshments for six focus groups on November 1 and 2, 1988. Audio and videotaping of the groups will be required.
