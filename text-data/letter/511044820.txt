Interoffice Memorandum

Subject: TCRC Presentation - Celanese Date: September 27, 1984

To: Distribution From: Mr. E. C. Jones

On October 4, 1984, at 10:30 A.M. in the Dining Room of Building
61-1, Dr. Lance Deutsch will present the results of a joint RJR-
Celanese study on the parameters affecting the softening of filters
during smoking (hot collapse). This study covers the effects of total
denier, denier per filament, plasticizer level, ventilation and crimp
index.

Please invite those in your area who might be interested.



E. C. Jones
