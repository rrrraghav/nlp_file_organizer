July 3, 1985

To: T. B. Owen
From: B. Wallace

Subject: SALEM - Phoenix Magazine Pop-Up

This forwards for your review and approval comprehensive layouts for subject Phoenix Program.

Background

As you are aware, Phoenix is a SALEM FF Box line extension designed to have specific appeal to the younger adult smoker. Phoenix is scheduled to be introduced in the Philadelphia Region August 5, 1985.

Current total Promotion spending is estimated at $2,494M with 29MM units of promoted volume (see Attachment I for details). The program described below, will cost $92SM, both General and Black Markets and will generate 7.5MM units of promoted volume.

Program Objective

• Generate project Phoenix awareness trial, purchase and conversion.

Program Strategy

• Implement consumer purchase inducement program via targeted general market younger adult and Black smoker magazines.

Creative Strategy

• Maintain Phoenix campaign "look" via a non-traditional graphic format.

• Strongly communicate offer value utilizing invitational and relevant copy.

Offer Description

Magazine Page with Pop-Up (See Attachment II – Magazine List)

• Free Pack Coupon
• Mail-In Offer for free T-shirt
• Unannounced bounceback for two free cartons to identify competitive smokers.
