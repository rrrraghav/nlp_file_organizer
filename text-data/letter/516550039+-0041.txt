2356/Utilization of Stems and Dust

The KG-4 and C stems have been secured for the trial at the C. E. Bauer Company. The WT stems for this work will be generated from a test at No. 60 Processing. The stems and the equipment for moisture conditioning the stems, for refining, are scheduled for shipment January 7.

2394/Project NL

A. Laboratory Pentane Extractions

Three batches (100 gms. each) of scrap chewing tobacco were given a 1.00% ammoniation and extracted continuously for 60 minutes at ambient temperature and 200 ml./min. extract flow rate. Pentane was used to extract the tobacco, and aqueous malic acid was used to neutralize the nicotine. Average percent nicotine for the 3 batches was 0.082%.
