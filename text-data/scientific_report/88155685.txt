MONTHLY MANAGEMENT STATUS REPORT PROJECT NO.: C-194

PROJECT TITLE: TOBACCO FLAVOR - 1981 DATE: Dec.31, 1981
BUDGET: $394,670

PROJECT DESCRIPTION: A variety of flavorants will be investigated. Primarily we will deal with
specific flavor improvers, extenders, enhancers and replacements for
tobacco flavors. The project involves synthesis, planning and
execution of synthesis followed by preliminary screening for utility.
Initial safety evaluation will be performed. Further work in sensory
evaluation will be conducted. Successful evaluation by all groups would
lead to further development. Computer applications relating to basic flavor research
and for flavorant record keeping purposes.
