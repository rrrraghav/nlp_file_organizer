Protocol for Enzyme Immunoassay of Nicotine

Introduction

As an alternative to RIA of nicotine, enzyme immunoassay (EIA)
is sought in order to avoid some of the disadvantages of RIA currently
available, i.e. short half-life of radiolabeled compound and its
cost involved, potential radiation hazard, etc.

EIA could be as sensitive as RIA and the assay speed could be
equally fast and efficient. Here, described the development of an
enzyme immunoassay of nicotine using Î²-galactosidase as an enzyme
of choice.
