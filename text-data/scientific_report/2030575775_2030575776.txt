NOTES ON PARK 500 WASTE WATER TREATMENT
SLUDGE PRODUCTION AND DISPOSAL:

- AVERAGE DUMPSTER WEIGHT: 10 TONS @ 60% FULL

- AVERAGE NUMBER OF DUMPSTERS/DAY : 7-10
- POSSIBLE NUMBER OF DUMPSTERS/DAY : 15+

- DENSITY OF SLUDGE : COMPACTED - 55 LBS/FT³
UNCOMPACTED - 35 LBS/FT³
ESTIMATED TYPICAL DUMPSTER - 40-45 LBS/FT³

- DUMPSTER SIZES: 3 = 20 CUBIC YARD DUMPSTERS
3 = 20 CUBIC YARD DUMPSTERS

NOTES ON TYPICAL OPERATION SCHEDULE:

- WASTEWATER PLANT RUNS 24 HOURS A DAY EVERY
DAY INCLUDING HOLIDAYS.
- SLUDGE PRODUCTION OCCURS AT ALL HOURS; TRUCKS
MUST BE AVAILABLE TO HAUL DUMPSTERS AT ANY
GIVEN TIME...
- SLUDGE PRODUCTION CAN LAST FOR 36 STRAIGHT
HOURS; ALSO, THE SLUDGE PRESS IS RARELY
SHUT DOWN FOR MORE THAN 8 HOURS AT A
TIME.
- DUMPSTERS ARE TYPICALLY FILLED EVERY 3 HOURS,
BUT UNDER HIGH SLUDGE PRODUCTION RATES,
DUMPSTERS MAY BE FULL IN 1 ½ – 2 HOURS.

NOTES ON SLUDGE LANDFILLING:

- SLUDGE CONTAINS NO HEAVY METALS OR PESTICIDES
