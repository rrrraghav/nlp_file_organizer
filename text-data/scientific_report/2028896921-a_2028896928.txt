ABSTRACT

In the late 1920’s, “Field” (the first mentholated cigarettes) was marketed. Following early efforts of peppermint and floral brands in the 1930’s, the initial marketing considerations began in the mid-1940's. The field continued to grow until the introduction of Viceroy cigarettes. Up until that time, other mentholated cigarettes were offered to the public with relatively small market shares. In the 1970’s and 1980’s, approximately 36% of division to total cigarette sales. Account for about 25% of the total cigarette sales.
