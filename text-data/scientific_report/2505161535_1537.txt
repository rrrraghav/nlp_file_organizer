EXPERIMENT

Effects of diluted smoke with added acetaldehyde and of diluted
acetaldehyde vapor on the respiratory tract.
Animals: CD-rats, 40 male and 40 female per group, age at start
of inhalation approx. 52 d.
cigarettes: 2R1; dilution 1:60; TPM: 0.53 mg/l; nicotine: 31.40 /l;
CO: 322 ppm; acetaldehyde from 21 to 599 ppm.
exposure: 420 min/d, 5 consecutive days with 3-days training.
dissections after 7, 14 and 21 d.
