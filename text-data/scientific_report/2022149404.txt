PROGRAM TITLE: POST EXPANSION HEAT TREATMENT

OBJECTIVE: Determine commercial feasibility of post expansion
heat treatment of DIET and ET.

PROGRAM APPROACH AND TIMING:

A. Complete subjective evaluation of PEHT DIET. Order
prototype unit if subjectives are acceptable. 1st Qtr.

B. Determine preferred reordering technique to minimize
CV loss. 2nd Qtr.

C. Install prototype PEHT unit. 3rd Qtr.

D. Develop process specifications. 4th Qtr.
