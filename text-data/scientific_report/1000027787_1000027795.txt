LFAD - AN ENVIRONMENTAL POLLUTANT

Submitted by: Dr. C. I. Lewis

Report number: Date: 11/29/73

Summary or Abstract:

This is a summary of a literature survey about lead in the environment. An estimate of the contribution of lead in cigarette tobacco to the overall environment has been made. It can be concluded that food is by far the greatest source of lead for man. Cigarette smoking is a minor contributor to the human body burden.
