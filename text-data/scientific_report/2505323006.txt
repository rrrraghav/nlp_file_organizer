Initiative: Scientific Issues – Technical Gaps Response Initiative

Project name: Sensory versus central pharmacologic effects of nicotine on the visually evoked potential

Objective: Investigate the time course of central reaction to inhaled puffs of cigarette smoke and compare to throat stimulation by nicotine vapors to provide sound scientific data on the routes of action of biologically active smoke components like nicotine (by 4th quarter 2000)

Project Leader: Hans-Jürgen Haussmann (Michael Kaegler)

Tactics and Milestones:

(1) Planning and preparation of experiments (by 3rd quarter 1999)
(2) Experiments and evaluation (by 2nd quarter 2000)
(3) Submission of abstract for poster presentation for ECRO 2000 (by 2nd quarter 2000)
(4) Preparation of full paper manuscript for publication (by 4th quarter 2000)
