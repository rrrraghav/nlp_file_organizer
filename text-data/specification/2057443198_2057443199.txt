VI. FIRST AID MEASURES
Eye Contact: Flush with water for 15 minutes. Get medical attention.
Skin Contact: Wash with soap and water.
Inhalation: Remove to fresh air. If not breathing, give artificial respiration.
Ingestion: Do not induce vomiting. Get medical attention immediately.
VII. SPILL or LEAK PROCEDURES
Small Spills: Sweep up and place in a suitable container for disposal.
Large Spills: Flush with water. Prevent from entering sewers or waterways.
VIII. HANDLING and STORAGE
Store in a cool, dry, well-ventilated area away from incompatible materials. Keep containers tightly closed.
IX. ADDITIONAL INFORMATION
This material is classified as an oxidizer and may cause fire if mixed with combustible materials.
