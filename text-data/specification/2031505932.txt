VI. FIRST AID

Eyes are most affected. Immediately flush eyes with water for
at least 15 minutes, occasionally lifting upper and lower eyelids.
Remove contact lenses if present. Get medical attention immediately.
Ingestion: Drink 1-2 glasses of milk or water and obtain immediate
medical attention. Do not induce vomiting.

VII. SPILL AND DISPOSAL PROCEDURES

In case of spill or leakage, dilute with water. Pour down the drain
following all applicable state and local regulations.
