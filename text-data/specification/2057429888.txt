FIRST AID MEASURES

INHALATION: IF INHALED, REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF NOT BREATHING, GIVE ARTIFICIAL
RESPIRATION. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION IMMEDIATELY.
SKIN CONTACT: FLUSH WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES WHILE REMOVING CONTAMINATED
CLOTHING AND SHOES. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION IMMEDIATELY.
EYE CONTACT: FLUSH WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES, OCCASIONALLY LIFTING UPPER
AND LOWER EYELIDS. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION IMMEDIATELY.
INGESTION: IF SWALLOWED, DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING.  GIVE LARGE QUANTITIES OF WATER.
GET MEDICAL ATTENTION IMMEDIATELY.

FIRE FIGHTING MEASURES

SPECIAL FIREFIGHTING PROCEDURES: WEAR SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS AND
PROTECTIVE CLOTHING TO PREVENT CONTACT WITH SKIN AND EYES.
UNUSUAL FIRE AND EXPLOSION HAZARDS:  MAY EMIT OXIDES OF NITROGEN DURING THERMAL
DECOMPOSITION.

ACCIDENTAL RELEASE MEASURES

PERSONAL PROTECTION: WEAR APPROPRIATE PERSONAL PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT, SEE SECTION 8.
ENVIRONMENTAL PRECAUTIONS: PREVENT ENTRY INTO WATERWAYS, SEWERS, OR EFFICIENT
CONTAINMENT SYSTEMS.
METHODS FOR CONTAINING AND CLEANING UP SPILLS:  ABSORB WITH INERT MATERIAL (E.G.,
SAND, VERMICULITE). COLLECT AND PLACE IN A SUITABLE CONTAINER FOR DISPOSAL.

HANDLING AND STORAGE

STORAGE TEMPERATURE: ROOM
SPECIFIC STORAGE REQUIREMENTS: KEEP CONTAINER TIGHTLY CLOSED. STORE IN A COOL, DRY,
WELL-VENTILATED AREA AWAY FROM INCOMPATIBLE MATERIALS.
OTHER PRECAUTIONS:  AVOID CONTACT WITH SKIN AND EYES.  WASH THOROUGHLY AFTER
HANDLING.
