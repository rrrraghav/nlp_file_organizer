SECTION III - PHYSICAL AND CHEMICAL DATA
BOILING POINT: 573°F (301°C)
MELTING POINT: Not Available
VAPOR PRESSURE: <0.001 mmHg @ 20°C
VAPOR DENSITY: >1 (Air = 1)
SOLUBILITY IN WATER: Miscible
SPECIFIC GRAVITY: 1.11
APPEARANCE: Clear, colorless liquid

SECTION IV - FIRE AND EXPLOSION HAZARD DATA
FLASH POINT: Not Applicable
AUTO-IGNITION TEMPERATURE: Not Available
FLAMMABLE LIMITS (in air): LEL: Not Available UEL: Not Available
EXTINGUISHING MEDIA: Use any extinguishing media suitable for surrounding fire.

SECTION V - HEALTH HAZARD DATA
ROUTE OF EXPOSURE: Inhalation, ingestion, skin and eye contact.
EFFECTS OF ACUTE OVEREXPOSURE:
INHALATION: May cause respiratory irritation.
INGESTION: May cause gastrointestinal distress.
SKIN CONTACT: Prolonged or repeated contact may cause mild irritation.
EYE CONTACT: May cause mild irritation.
EFFECTS OF CHRONIC OVEREXPOSURE: No known chronic effects.

SECTION VI - PRECAUTIONS FOR SAFE HANDLING AND USE
Avoid contact with skin and eyes. Use with adequate ventilation. Keep container closed.

SECTION VII - FIRST AID MEASURES
INHALATION: If inhaled, remove to fresh air.
INGESTION: If ingested, drink water. Call a physician if symptoms develop.
SKIN CONTACT: Wash affected area with soap and water.
EYE CONTACT: Flush eyes with water for 15 minutes.

SECTION VIII - STABILITY AND REACTIVITY DATA
STABLE: Yes
CONDITIONS TO AVOID: Excessive heat.
MATERIALS TO AVOID: Strong oxidizing agents.
HAZARDOUS DECOMPOSITION PRODUCTS: Carbon monoxide and carbon dioxide.

SECTION IX - SPILL OR LEAK PROCEDURES
Spills will form a slippery surface. Contain spill. Absorb with inert material. Collect and dispose of properly.

SECTION X - WASTE DISPOSAL METHOD
Dispose of in accordance with federal, state and local regulations.

SECTION XI – TRANSPORTATION INFORMATION
DOT PROPER SHIPPING NAME: Not Regulated
DOT HAZARD CLASS: None
DOT LABEL REQUIRED: None
DOT REPORTABLE QUANTITY: None

SECTION XII – ADDITIONAL INFORMATION
This material is not considered hazardous under OSHA Hazard Communication Standard (29 CFR 156.8).

NOTE: The information in this Material Safety Data Sheet should be used to provide guidance on the safe handling, use, and disposal of this product. It does not constitute a warranty and does not guarantee against any hazards that may arise from the use of this product.
