HEALTH HAZARD DATA

ACUTE EFFECTS:
HARMFUL IF SWALLOWED, INHALED OR ABSORBED THROUGH SKIN.
VAPOR OR MIST IRRITATING TO THE EYES, NOSE, MEMBRANES AND UPPER
RESPIRATORY TRACT.

FIRST AID MEASURES:
EYES: FLUSH WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES WHILE OPENING AND
CLOSING EYELIDS. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION IMMEDIATELY.
SKIN: WASH AREA WITH SOAP AND WATER. IF IRRITATION PERSISTS, GET
MEDICAL ATTENTION.
INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR.  IF NOT BREATHING GIVE ARTIFICIAL
RESPIRATION BY TRAINED PERSONNEL ONLY.  IF BREATHING IS DIFFICULT
GIVE OXYGEN. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.
INGESTION: DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING UNLESS DIRECTED BY PHYSICIAN.
GET MEDICAL ATTENTION IMMEDIATELY.



CHRONIC EFFECTS:
NO DATA AVAILABLE

CARCINOGENICITY:  OSHA, NTP AND ACGIH HAVE NO INFORMATION REGARDING
THE CARCINOGENICITY OF THIS CHEMICAL.

MUTAGENICITY:  NO TEST DATA AVAILABLE.

REPRODUCTIVE TOXICITY:  NO ADEQUATE STUDIES IN MAMMALS HAVE BEEN
CONDUCTED TO EVALUATE THE REPRODUCTIVE EFFECTS OF 2-HEXENAL.



PREVENTATIVE MEASURES:

WEAR APPROPRIATE PERSONAL PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT, SUCH AS SAFETY GLASSES,
GLOVES AND RESPIRATOR.  USE ONLY IN A WELL VENTILATED AREA.  AVOID
CONTACT WITH SKIN AND EYES.  KEEP CONTAINER TIGHTLY CLOSED.
