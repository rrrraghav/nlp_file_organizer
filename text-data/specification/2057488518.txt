HEALTH HAZARD DATA

TLV AND SOURCE:
MOLYBDENUM TRIOXIDE (MOO3) AS MOLYBDATE
ACGIH-TLV: 5MG/M3; 26974
CAPABILITIES: SKIN IRRITATION, EYE IRRITATION AND UPPER RESPIRATORY TRACT IRRITATION.

CHRONIC EXPOSURE MAY CAUSE DAMAGE TO IDENTIFIED THROUGH SKIN.

ACUTE EFFECTS: INHALATION WILL CAUSE IRRITATION AND UPPER
RESPIRATORY TRACT IRRITATION.

FIRST AID:
IN CASE OF CONTACT WITH EYES, FLUSH WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES AND CALL A PHYSICIAN.
IN CASE OF SKIN CONTACT, WASH AREA WITH SOAP AND WATER.
IF INHALED, REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF NOT BREATHING GIVE OXYGEN.
IF SWALLOWED, DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. CALL A PHYSICIAN IMMEDIATELY.

FIRE FIGHTING:
USE DRY CHEMICAL POWDER, CARBON DIOXIDE, OR APPROPRIATE FOAM. UNUSUAL FIRE AND EXPLOSION HAZARDS MAY OCCUR.
WEAR SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS AND PROTECTIVE CLOTHING TO PREVENT CONTACT WITH SKIN AND EYES.

STABILITY: STABLE UNDER NORMAL CONDITIONS. AVOID STRONG OXIDIZING AGENTS.
HAZARDOUS POLYMERIZATION: WILL NOT OCCUR.

HANDLING AND STORAGE:
STORE IN TIGHTLY CLOSED CONTAINERS. PROTECT FROM MOISTURE. KEEP AWAY FROM HEAT.
