FIRST AID MEASURES:
INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF NOT BREATHING, GIVE OXYGEN. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.
SKIN CONTACT: FLUSH WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES. REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.
EYE CONTACT: FLUSH WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.
INGESTION: DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. GIVE LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION IMMEDIATELY.

FIRE FIGHTING MEASURES:
EXTINGUISHING MEDIA: USE DRY CHEMICAL, CARBON DIOXIDE, OR FOAM.
SPECIAL FIREFIGHTING PROCEDURES: WEAR SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS AND PROTECTIVE CLOTHING. AVOID CONTACT WITH SKIN AND EYES.
UNUSUAL FIRE AND EXPLOSION HAZARDS: MAY REACT VIOLENTLY WITH COMBUSTIBLE MATERIALS.

STABILITY AND REACTIVITY DATA:
INCOMPATIBILITIES: STRONG REDUCING AGENTS, ORGANIC MATTER, METALS.
HAZARDOUS DECOMPOSITION PRODUCTS: CHROMIUM OXIDES.

SPILL OR LEAK PROCEDURES:
VENTILATE AREA. WEAR APPROPRIATE PERSONAL PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT. CONTAIN SPILL. ABSORB WITH INERT MATERIAL. COLLECT AND PLACE IN A SUITABLE CONTAINER FOR DISPOSAL. FLUSH AREA WITH WATER.

HANDLING AND STORAGE:
STORE IN COOL, DRY, WELL-VENTILATED AREA. KEEP AWAY FROM INCOMPATIBLE MATERIALS. HANDLE WITH CARE.
