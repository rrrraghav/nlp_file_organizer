HMIS HEALTH RATING 1 FLAMMABILITY 0 REACTIVITY 0
Amorphous powder, White / Off-white. Odorless
Mild eye irritant. Mild skin irritant. Mild respiratory tract
irritant.
The powder can explode if mixed in air and ignited in a confined
space. Under hot ambient conditions or if heated, flammable
methanol and methylacetate are released. The powder over time
can produce an explosive atmosphere in a closed space.
Transport and storage tanks and will give rise to Class A
fire. If unconfined, ignition of the powder will explosion limited.
In case of fire use Water streams.
