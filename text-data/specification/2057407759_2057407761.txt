HEALTH HAZARD DATA
ACUTE EFFECTS: EXPOSURE MAY CAUSE IRRITATION TO THE MUCOUS MEMBRANES AND UPPER
RESPIRATORY TRACT.  MAY BE HARMFUL IF SWALLOWED, INHALED OR ABSORBED THROUGH SKIN.
CHRONIC EFFECTS: NO CHRONIC DATA AVAILABLE.
NOTE: SEE SECTION 11 FOR MORE DETAILED INFORMATION.

FIRST AID MEASURES
IN CASE OF CONTACT WITH EYES, FLUSH WITH PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES
AND SEEK MEDICAL ATTENTION.  IF INHALED, REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF SWALLOWED, DO NOT
INDUCE VOMITING. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION IMMEDIATELY.  FOR SKIN CONTACT, WASH AREA
WITH SOAP AND WATER.

FIRE FIGHTING MEASURES
FLASH POINT: 107°F (42°C) METHOD: TAG CLOSED CUP
LOWER LIMIT: ND UPPER LIMIT: ND
EXTINGUISHING MEDIA: USE DRY CHEMICAL POWDER, CARBON DIOXIDE, OR APPROPRIATE FOAM.
SPECIAL FIREFIGHTING PROCEDURES: WEAR SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS AND PROTECTIVE
CLOTHING TO PREVENT CONTACT WITH SKIN AND EYES.

ACCIDENTAL RELEASE MEASURES
AVOID RELEASE TO THE ENVIRONMENT.  WEAR APPROPRIATE PERSONAL PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT, SUCH AS
GLOVES, SAFETY GLASSES, AND RESPIRATORY PROTECTION.  CONTAIN SPILL AND COLLECT FOR
DISPOSAL IN ACCORDANCE WITH APPLICABLE REGULATIONS.

HANDLING AND STORAGE
STORE IN A COOL, DRY, WELL-VENTILATED AREA. KEEP CONTAINER TIGHTLY CLOSED.
AVOID CONTACT WITH STRONG OXIDIZING AGENTS.

ENGINEERING CONTROLS
MEANS SHOULD BE PROVIDED TO PREVENT THE BUILDUP OF STATIC ELECTRICITY.

PERSONAL PROTECTION
WEAR APPROPRIATE PERSONAL PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT, SUCH AS GLOVES, SAFETY GLASSES, AND
RESPIRATORY PROTECTION.

DATA NOT AVAILABLE.

STABILITY AND REACTIVITY
