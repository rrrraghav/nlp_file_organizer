SECTION V
HAZARDOUS INGREDIENTS

A. A HAZARDOUS INGREDIENT IS ONE WHICH
MEETS ONE OR MORE OF THE FOLLOWING
CRITERIA:

1. IT IS LISTED IN THE ANNUAL SUBSTANCE
REGISTRY AND MEETS THE CRITERIA FOR
REPORTING, OR IT IS KNOWN TO BE TOXIC
AND THE PARAMETERS OF THAT
REGISTRY.

AND/OR

2. IT HAS A ESTABLISHED 8-HOUR
TIME WEIGHTED AVERAGE (TWA) EXPOSURE
LIMIT AS DEFINED BY THE OCCUPATIONAL
SAFETY AND HEALTH ADMINISTRATION, OR
THE U.S. ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION
AGENCY, OR BY STATE INDUSTRIAL HYGIENE
REGULATIONS, AND ITS CONCENTRATION
EXCEEDS THAT VALUE, OR IF THE PRODUCT
IS A CARCINOGEN, IT IS PRESENT AT 1%
OR MORE.

AND/OR

3. IT CONTRIBUTES TO HAZARD OF THE
PRODUCT AS A WHOLE.

EYE CONTACT:  MAY CAUSE IRRITATION.

SKIN CONTACT:  PROLONGED OR REPEATED
CONTACT MAY CAUSE IRRIATION AND DERMA-

INTESTINAL DISTRESS, NAUSEA AND VOMITING.
DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. GET MEDICAL
ATTENTION IMMEDIATELY.

SECTION VIII
PRECAUTIONS FOR SAFE HANDLING AND USE

AVOID CONTACT WITH SKIN, EYES, AND CLOTHING.
USE WITH ADEQUATE VENTILATION. KEEP AWAY
FROM HEAT, SPARKS, OPEN FLAME, AND OTHER
IGNITION SOURCES.  STORE IN A COOL, DRY
PLACE.
