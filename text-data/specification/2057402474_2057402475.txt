SECTION VII – FIRST AID MEASURES
EYES: Immediately flush with large amounts of water for at least 15 minutes, occasionally lifting upper and lower eyelids. Get medical attention.
SKIN: Wash affected area with soap and water. Remove contaminated clothing. If irritation persists, get medical attention.
INGESTION: Do NOT induce vomiting. Dilute by giving plenty of water or milk. Get immediate medical attention.
INHALATION: Remove victim to fresh air. Administer oxygen if breathing is difficult. Get medical attention.
