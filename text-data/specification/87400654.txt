The pH of a 50% concentration of B164 in water was theoretically calculated to
be 4.82 at 23°C according to the extrapolation procedure of Dr. P. D. Schickedanz
Loriillard Research Center Accession Number 1662.

not applicable

SOLUBILITY
MUTAGENICITY SOLVENTS
B164 forms a slightly soluble slurry in distilled water at 0.2 g/mL, with stirring
at room temperature. Reference BC30-62.

PH
B164 is insoluble for IP dosing. Reference BC20-48.

ORAL
B164 forms a suspension in corn oil at 0.1 g/mL, with constant stirring. Dosing
volumes of 20 mL/kg or higher may be necessary to achieve desired dosage.
Reference BC20-48.
*Pulverize with mortar & pestle before using preparing dosing solutions.

ACUTE CARDIOVASCULAR
B164 in insoluble for this procedure. Reference BC20-48.

STORAGE RECOMMENDATIONS
Refrigerate in an amber glass bottle at no more than 8°C.
