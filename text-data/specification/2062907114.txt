 Indirect Food Additives Regulation 21
 CFR 175.105 "Adhesives".


OPERATING CONDITIONS: Direct transfer from glue wheel or
 stencil. Can also be extruded or sprayed.

     1. Stir thoroughly before using.

     2. Ready-for-use viscosity as supplied.
        May be diluted with water.

     3. Clean-up: Wet film and dry film
         soluble in warm water.



PRECAUTIONS: Protect from temperatures below 40F.
              Rotate stock using oldest material first
              Keep covered to prevent drying out and
              contamination.
              Do not mix with other adhesives.
