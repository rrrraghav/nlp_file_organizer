SECTION V - HEALTH HAZARD DATA

Effects of Exposure: highly toxic, carcinogen

Swallowing: May be fatal if swallowed.
Skin absorption: May be fatal if absorbed through skin.
Inhalation: May be fatal if inhaled. Do not inhale dust.
Skin contact: Do not get on skin. Wash thoroughly after handling.
Eye contact: Do not get in eyes.

Emergency and First Aid Procedures:

Swallowing: Call a physician immediately.
Skin: Flush skin with copious amounts of water for at least 15 min.
Remove contaminated clothing and shoes.
Call a physician.
Inhalation: Move to fresh air. If not breathing give artificial respiration.
Eyes: Flush eyes with copious amounts of water for at least 15 min.
Call a physician.
