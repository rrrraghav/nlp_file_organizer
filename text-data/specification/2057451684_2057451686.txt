SPECIAL FIRE FIGHTING PROCEDURES
Use self-contained breathing apparatus. Allow fire to burn under supervision. Use water spray for cooling fire exposed containers.

Reactivity Data
Polymerization will not occur.
CONDITIONS TO AVOID: High temperatures, contact with oxidizing agents.
INCOMPATIBILITY: Strong oxidizers.
HAZARDOUS DECOMPOSITION PRODUCTS: Carbon monoxide and carbon dioxide.

TOXICOLOGICAL INFORMATION
Acute Effects: Prolonged or repeated skin contact may cause dermatitis.
Chronic Effects: None known.
Carcinogenicity: No components are listed by NTP, IARC, or OSHA.
PRIMARY ROUTES OF EXPOSURE: Skin Contact, Inhalation, Eye Contact.

PREVENTATIVE MEASURES
Eye Protection: Safety glasses with side shields or goggles.
Ventilation: Use adequate ventilation.
Respiratory Protection: None required under normal conditions.
Gloves: Wear chemical resistant gloves.
Other Protective Equipment: None required.

FIRST AID MEASURES
Eye Contact: Flush with water for 15 minutes. Get medical attention.
Skin Contact: Wash with soap and water.
Inhalation: Remove to fresh air.
Ingestion: Do not induce vomiting. Get medical attention.

HANDLING AND STORAGE
Store in a cool, dry place. Keep containers closed.
