SECTION V - FIRE & EXPLOSION HAZARD DATA

SPECIAL FIREFIGHTING PROCEDURES: USE SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS TO AVOID EXPOSURE. COOL CONTAINERS WITH WATER.
UNUSUAL FIRE AND EXPLOSION HAZARDS: DUST IS EXPLOSIVE WHEN DISPERSED IN AIR.

SECTION VI - HEALTH HAZARD DATA

EFFECTS OF OVEREXPOSURE: NONE REPORTED.
PRIMARY ROUTES OF EXPOSURE: INHALATION, EYE CONTACT, SKIN CONTACT, INGESTION.
EYE CONTACT: MAY CAUSE IRRITATION.
SKIN CONTACT: PROLONGED OR REPEATED CONTACT MAY CAUSE MILD IRRITATION.
INHALATION: NO SIGNIFICANT HAZARD REPORTED.
INGESTION: LOW TOXICITY.

SECTION VII - PRECAUTIONS FOR SAFE HANDLING AND USE

VENTILATE AREA. AVOID DUST FORMATION. CLEAN UP SPILLS IMMEDIATELY.
STORE IN COOL, DRY PLACE. KEEP CONTAINER TIGHTLY CLOSED.
AVOID CONTACT WITH STRONG ACIDS AND OXIDIZING AGENTS.
