Local regulations regarding waste disposal. Do not

VIII. SPECIAL PROTECTION INFORMATION

Eye & Face Protection: Wear safety glasses or goggles where eye contact may occur.

Respiratory Protection: Use mechanical ventilation or respiratory protection when handling this product.

Ventilation: Provide adequate ventilation.

Gloves: Wear chemical resistant gloves.

IX. FIRST AID MEASURES

Eye Contact: Flush with water for 15 minutes. Get medical attention.
Skin Contact: Wash with soap and water.
Inhalation: Remove to fresh air. If not breathing, administer artificial respiration. Get medical attention.
Ingestion: Do not induce vomiting. Get medical attention immediately.
