SUBJECT: CONDITION LETTER - July / August / September 1996

Lorillard Sales Trends:

HARLEY DAVIDSON: Continue to sell with coupon support and buy down program. Maverick presell is going very well at this time.

NEWPORT: Newport family continue to gain sales in Tulsa, as of 8/31/96 Newport is up 746 cases for a increase of 49.0%. Newport box king and box 100’s sales continue to grow.

STYLE: Sales are flat even with coupon support. We are not gaining new trial smokers that are switching to other competitive brands.

COMPETITIVE SALES TRENDS:

P.M. - Promoting Basic with attachment items and coupons. Marlboro sales continue to be strong with their flex fund program.

R.J.R. - Camel Red is expanding to the Tulsa Market in September. Camel, Winston, and Basic continue to be supported with buy down’s and attachment items. Total industry sales in Tulsa for R.J.R. are down 1.75% in June. However Camel and Doral sales have increased during this period.

B&W - G.P.C. sales continue to grow with the buy down program. Kool's continue to be promoted with coupons, to convert Newport smokers to Kool’s. Additional Window border tape with Kool logo is being placed in high menthol areas.
