Introductory Allowance (See Attachment A for details):
The Introductory Allowance is $6.00 per 12M case for Parliament Full Flavor Kings (Regular & Menthol) and Parliament Menthol Lights Kings Box paid via a credit memo each week to wholesale accounts.
