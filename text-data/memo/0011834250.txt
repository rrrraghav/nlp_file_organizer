SUBJECT: BETA INTERNAL SAMPLES/267

To take advantage of available tobaccos from the STETSON and AMELIA
CPT products, plan to make ten BETA internal samples in Macon. These
samples will permit assessments of additional blends and designs as
potential BETA candidates, as well as allow us more experience running
the BETA fabrication equipment.

Tobaccos we will be using are: STETSON XLF-662T with XFP-357, XFP-375
and unflavored, and AMELIA XLF-680G unflavored. It is imperative that
the unflavored tobaccos be well blended.
