SUBJECT: BUCKS

Bucks, generic priced 85mm FF & LTS, began factory shipments December 18th and retail activity January 2nd in Birmingham.

Because Bucks is expected to compete with Winston and/or Camel, these markets were selected to represent one above average market for each of these brands. Both markets are better or better for 85mm price value brands (see below).

SUMMARY

• At the end of seven weeks (w/e 2/19), Bucks registers preliminary retail shares of 2.0 and 0.8 in Birmingham and Arizona, respectively with SDI adjusted shares of 1.9 and 0.7. In order to estimate national performance, shares are adjusted based on the two markets’ SDI's for the king size price value category.
