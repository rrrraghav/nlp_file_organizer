Subject: R&D Bulletin - Week of August 15-19, 1994

Brand X/Lighthouse
Brand X: A meeting was held on August 16 to discuss details relating to the November “mini test markets” in Portland and Denver. Current timelines for test market production are still accurate. Foil embossing rollers with the "Dave's" logo (as opposed to "Pull") have been ordered and will arrive on September 23. Production is scheduled to start September 26 at the Manufacturing Center. Production quantities are: 13.2 million FF; 10.8 million Lights; and 7.6 million promotional units (B1GIF, FF and Lights).

A “tens pack” for FF and Lights, direct mail promotion, will also be produced. The scheduled production start date is October 17 in Louisville. Production quantity will be 1-2 million.

PRODUCT MODIFICATIONS - EXPORT
Bond Street KS SP & L&M LS FTB (Russia): The cigarette specifications for Bond Street KS SP and L&M LS FTB have been revised incorporating a modified (reduced cost) blend. The revised specifications have been written and transmitted to Manufacturing Specifications. L&M LS FTB production start-up began 8/15/94 at the Manufacturing Center. Bond Street KS SP production start-up is scheduled to begin the week of 8/22/94 at the Manufacturing Center.
