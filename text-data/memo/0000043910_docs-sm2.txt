SUBJECT: VICEROY Rich Lights Sampling Program - Phase 1.

1. Beginning on Monday, November 27, and running through Thursday, December 14, VICEROY Rich Lights will conduct the first phase of its street-corner sampling program. Full packs of Kings and 100’s will be distributed in 14 VICEROY Extra Mild “A” markets, plus Las Vegas. See attached list for details.

2. Included with each pack of VICEROY Rich Lights (Kings and 100's) will be a mail-in refund certificate good on the purchase of a carton of the new product.

3. This memo is to advise all departments concerned of the requirements involved in the sampling program.
