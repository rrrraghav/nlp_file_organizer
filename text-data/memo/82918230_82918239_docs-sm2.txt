Re: MSA NATIONAL DATA SUMMARY - MARCH, 1991

Enclosed is a set of tables summarizing key national volume and share trends based on MSA industry shipment data through March 1991. These formats provide comprehensive overviews of major market segments which are itemized in the Table of Contents. In each segment, all brands with a share of .01% or more for the most recent 12-month period are listed in order of importance. Data are shown for total 1990, current and year-ago 12-month and year-to-date periods, and the latest five consecutive 3-month periods.
