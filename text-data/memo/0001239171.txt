RE: COMPETITIVE CIGARETTE SALES/ADVERTISING COMPARISONS

Attached are comparisons of cigarette advertising expenditures
for the third quarter 1969 compared to the third quarter 1968.

In addition, also attached are Sales/Advertising comparisons by
brand for the period ending September 30, 1969.  Sales and
advertising comparisons on an individual brand basis are shown
for the total market, with separate breakdowns including an
individual breakdown for Over 85mm Filters (non-menthol) and Over
85mm Menthol Filters.

All sales figures for this report are derived from our Jobber
reports and advertising expenditures are reported traceable
advertising dollars.

As an additional point of references and information, listed below
are cigarette advertising expenditures by company for the twelve
months ending September 30, 1969 with the percent change.
Also shown is the percent change in company sales for
the twelve months ending September 1969.
