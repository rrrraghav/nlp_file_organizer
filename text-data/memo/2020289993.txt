Lights) were submitted to Semiworx.

Regular in princess packs.

Models have been designed for Full Flavor and Lights at circumferences of 23,
24, and 24.8, with POL prototypes made and being tested by CTSD.

Initial models were requested from Semiworx using Marlboro and Bucks
blends.
