TOTAL INDUSTRY

Total industry unit shipment volume for the week ending Friday, April 24, 1998 was 9,066B units.

This volume is -.225B units or -2.7% versus the same week, year ago.

Industry shipment volume appeared to “recover” as compared to the prior two weeks due to more representative buying by McLean Distributing (see chart on the next page).

Generally speaking, there were no other significant or unusual events affecting industry shipments this past week.

For the year-to-date industry shipments, through Friday, April 24 total 135.405B units.

This volume is 4.763B units or -3.4% versus year ago, year-to-date.
