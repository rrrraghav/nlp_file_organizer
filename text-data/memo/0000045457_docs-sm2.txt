February 19, 1972

The 56th Product Development Committee Meeting will be held at 9:00 A.M.
on Wednesday, February 23, 1972, in the Advertising Conference room.

Following is the meeting agenda:

I. PDC approval of minutes covering Meeting Report #54.

II. The Chairman of ad hoc committees will report on each PDC project.
This report will include, if possible, the current status of the project,
next anticipated step(s) toward completion of that project, and its
projected date of final completion. Further, it ad hoc committee
feels that the description or objective of any project should be received,
this subject should also be included during the discussion of that project.

III. New business will be entertained from committee members following
discussion of the preceding items.

Please note: The attachment to this agenda is a roster of all existing ad hoc
committees.
