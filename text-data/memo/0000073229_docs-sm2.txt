SUBJECT: DUPONT ACCEPTABILITY RATINGS OF MENTHOL 100 GEMINI CPT CANDIDATES/267

Attached are Dupont acceptability results for the Menthol 100 GEMINI CPT candidates.

Results show:

* The two GEMINI candidates are about equally acceptable.

* The candidates were less acceptable than Salem Lights.

* The candidates were more acceptable than True.

Results from the other GEMINI CPT candidate tests were reported earlier (Internal Correspondence, Kirby to Kohnhhorst, 6/13/84) and showed the same pattern of results.

TEST DETAILS

Like the other GEMINI tests at Dupont, representative smokers of lights/mild competitive brands were used. They included smokers of cork (C) or white (W) tipped Menthol 100 products which are listed below:

Salem Lights (W) Vantage (C)
B&H Lights (W) Bright (W)
Merit (C) Slims Lights (W) Eve Lights (W)
Virginia (W) Satin (W)
