Hr. R. S. Sprinkle, III

J. G. Brooks

In-Laboratory Cigarette Panels L84-61A, B, C, and D
GCS-PILOT Blend (Control) * VS GCS-2RA, -4A, -6A, and -7A

The above cigarettes were evaluated by members of the Laboratory
panel. Panelists were given a known control and test cigarette
and were asked to comment on differences observed while smoking.
Panelists were also asked which model was preferred. Results are:

1. L84-61A: Control VS GCS-2RA

Preference
Control 5
GCS-2RA 3
No Choice 0

Verbatim Comments

Control - Smoother, slightly butter taste
Smoother, less finishing flavor taste
Heavy flavor taste, slightly heavy flavor aftertaste

GCS-2RA - More tobacco taste, less dry, more rounded/balanced
taste
Richer, fuller, less dry
Sweeter (styerx?), slightly less dry, less impact
More impact
Sweeter, more added flavor taste, slightly more
impact, slightly less dry, not as well-rounded
High flavor level, stronger, more impact
Milder, blander taste, more tobacco taste, slight
flavor taste - slightly spicy and sweet

Summary

GCS-2RA was perceived as being sweeter, more highly flavored, and
slightly less dry than the Control.
