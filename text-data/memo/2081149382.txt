The February Marlboro Milds Menthol BIG1F Pack Promotion will be conducted
in the Atlanta, GA, and Pittsburgh, PA, test markets beginning January 31, 2000.
The Milds Menthol program will be conducted in conjunction with the nationally
scheduled February Marlboro Menthol BIG1F Promotion. The product delivery
dates for this program will be November 29 through December 30, 2000.

The BIG1F product used in this promotion is composed of revenue and
complimentary 20’s in sleeves and packed in 6M cases. Each master case
holds 30 white cartons. Each carton contains five two-pack deals –
50% revenue and 50% complimentary. There are 150 two-pack units in
each case.

Billing instructions are as follows:

1. Give extra dating (a total of 30 days) to wholesalers.
2. Credit the complimentary cigarettes to sales.
