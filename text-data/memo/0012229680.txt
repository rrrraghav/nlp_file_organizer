SUBJECT SUMMARY OF SMOKE DELIVERIES - FIRST HALF 1980

The attached figures and tables summarize the particulate and gas phase smoke deliveries of B&W and competition brands analyzed during the first half of 1980.

Figures 1-3 are bar graphs that show the relative position of each brand for tar, nicotine, nitrogen oxides, carbon monoxide, hydrogen cyanide and acetaldehyde deliveries. Appendices I and II list average deliveries, number of determinations, standard deviations and ranges for each B&W brand. This type of information for competitive brands is listed in Appendix III. Brands showing changes of 1 mg tar or more since our last report (3/14/80), which summarized 1979 smoke deliveries, are shown in table 1.
