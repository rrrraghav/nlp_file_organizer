PROJECT: 234-61-01 (Quality) and 234-60-0 (Tooling)

SUBJECT: Process Modification to Minimize ECC Production Defect

DISCUSSION: It was concluded that any process modifications
should not be so technical that an all new modified
tooling production is required. The objective of
tooling production is to be achieved.

The current process modification will be implemented
and tested with 234-61-01 and 234-60-0 tooling.

1. The Virginia side will dry cleaned and the
linings will be left in.

2. The Burley side will be dry rolled and the
linings will be left in.

3. The Burley and Virginia sides will be dry rolled
and the linings will be left in.

ACTION: Mr. Mully will establish test dates, assign sample
numbers and request production of samples.
