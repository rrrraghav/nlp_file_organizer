SUBJECT: HII Test of Production Marlboro Red 85mm Soft Pack
versus
Production Winston Red 85mm Soft Pack
(Project # 2515/10152)

OVERVIEW

The Marlboro Red 85mm soft pack smokers significantly preferred (57%/3%) their own brand to Winston Red 85mm soft pack. Qualitatively, Marlboro was perceived to have a "milder taste", “easier draw”, to be “less harsh”,
"cooler smoking" and "not have a dry taste in the mouth”. A larger percentage of these smokers preferred Marlboro because they were milder and not as harsh.

The Winston Red 85mm soft pack smokers significantly preferred (56%/4%) their own brand to Marlboro Red 85mm soft pack. Qualitatively, Winston was perceived to be "more satisfying". Marlboro was perceived to be “milder tasting”.
