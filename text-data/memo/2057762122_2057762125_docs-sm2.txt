D8AXR = Red S2 and Black B4 = Capri 100
D8AXQ-1 = Red E4 and Black S2 = Trim 100 (Model 1)

The purpose of this POL test is to determine the consumer accept-
ability of Capri 100 versus Trim 100 (Model 1) cigarettes.

The Trim cigarettes (D8AXQ-1) were different in FTC tar and nicotine
deliveries, puff count, total RID, static burning time, cigarette length,
filter RID, filter length, filter weight, ventilation, total alkaloids,
and total reducing sugars.

These cigarettes will be overtipped and mailed to the panelists in
white packs.
