MEMORANDUM

August 1, 1979

TO: J. Platt
FROM: J. J. Tatulli
RE: Kent III 100’s 20’s Sampling

Down the street salesforce 20’s sampling will begin October 15, 1979.

The salesforce will sample for 5 weeks with 20’s, switch to 4’s for
4 weeks and back with 20’s for 3 weeks.

Please schedule into production the following quantities of Kent III 100’s
20’s packs:

477,000 20’s packs to arrive week of 10/1.
286,200 20’s packs to arrive week of 12/10.

These 20’s will contain a pack insert and will have the “Complimentary”
All Applicable Taxes Paid closure seal.

Material will arrive in Greensboro by 9/3/79.



JJT/ljb
cc: P. A. Lawless
