Subject: Weekly Report

I. Domestic Product Support:

Menthol:

B&H RS: No Change.

MOOG:

For both K and N types, Semi-works models were made with casing and aftercut modifications. Analyses are complete, subjectives are completed and a memo is being written.

General Studies: No change.

Project Levo:

Flavor modifications were subjectively evaluated by injection, prior to requesting models in small scale. Menthol in each case will be 100% Bagno versus 100% Sagno with additive. Differences were seen. Additional solutions will be evaluated.

II. Filter R&D:

New Filter Concepts: No activity.

III. Technology Support:

Release Compounds: No change.

CR Compound Evaluation: No change.

Menthol Release Compounds: No change.

A meeting was held to discuss plans for further development for itaconate compounds. Plans are:
1. Efforts will concentrate on the poly menthyl itaconate.
2. CR will pursue a minor synthesis variation.
3. A kilo of the poly compound will be produced (6 weeks).
4. Studies will be conducted to characterize application and delivery efficiency.
