WEEKLY OVERVIEW

LEAF

□ USA : All flue cured purchases and processing are now complete, with shipments commencing in mid October. Burley orders have been placed.

□ Malawi : Purchasing of both flue cured and burley grades has been completed, with processing expected to finish by September 22.

□ Zimbabwe : Purchasing of initial orders has been fulfilled, with processing expected to be completed by September 25. Purchasing of the “CHAHO” grade should be realised by week end. All shipments are on schedule.

□ Greece/Turkey : Orders for PMI’s oriental purchases have been placed with PM's representatives from these origins.

Representatives from both PM Australia and Richmond Agronomy will be making field inspections in North Queensland during the week commencing September 18.

TOBACCO PROCESSING



The Primary QI is continuing its good performance with an index of 9.4 for the week, being 9.3% above target.
