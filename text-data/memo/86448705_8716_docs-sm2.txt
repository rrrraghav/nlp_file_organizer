Total Industry

Total industry unit shipment volume for the week ending Friday, February 7, the fifth full week of 1997, was 8.810B units.

This volume is +.076B units or +.9% versus the same week, year ago.

Year-to-date through February 7, unit shipments are 43.760B units. This volume is -.4553B units or -.94% versus year ago, year-to-date.

Note that 1997 has, year-to-date, two fewer shipping days than did 1996. The current rate of decline for total shipments on a daily average rate basis through February 7 is just - .27%.
