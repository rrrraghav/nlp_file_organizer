Subject: Request for Production of Consumer Test Cigarettes
Purpose: To determine the consumer acceptability of current production Marlboro 85’s (2277) relative to Winston 85’s (10019). Both cigarettes are to be overtipped to the same butt length. The test will be conducted among both Marlboro smokers and the smokers of “85”/80mm non-menthol filters. It is suggested that both brands be purchased at retail, with some effort being made to control for age.
