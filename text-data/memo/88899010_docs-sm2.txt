RE: HARLEY-DAVIDSON EXPANSION -- MANUFACTURING COORDINATION MEETING

Review of Updated Volume Requirements
Review of Packaging Delivery Timing
Review of Manufacturing Scheduling

Your attendance is important; if you are unable to attend please call (x3405).
