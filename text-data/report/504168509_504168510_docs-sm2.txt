A FILTER FOR THE SELECTIVE
FILTRATION OF NICOTINE

SUMMARY:
Cacao bean hulls, when used as a filter for smoking materials, have
been shown to selectively remove nicotine from the mainstream smoke.

MEMORANDUM:

In conjunction with another study, cacao bean hull sheets were examined
as a filter material. Cigarettes were prepared from KF tobacco, 85 mm total
length, with a 42.5 mm filter made from cacao bean hull sheets. Suitable
controls were made entirely from KF tobacco. Table 1 shows date.
