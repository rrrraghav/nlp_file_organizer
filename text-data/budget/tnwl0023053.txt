CHECKLIST

Political Campaign Contribution Requests

CONTRIBUTIONS POLICY

1. Contributions may be made to individual candidate committees only:
   a. When legislature is not in session – OR –
   b. In response to fundraiser invitation (enclose copy)
2. Contributions to political organizations may be made as appropriate.
3. Contribution checks must be mailed from the Regional Office directly to the campaign committee or political organization with a transmittal letter from the Regional Vice President.
4. Regional Offices comply with all state laws and reporting requirements.
5. Checks not cashed within 30 days will be queried, checks will be canceled after 180 days.

This checklist must accompany all political campaign contributions requests. Use one checklist for each group of requests submitted for a single state at one time. Initial each statement or step as it is completed.
